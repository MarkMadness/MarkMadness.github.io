About Me

Who am I?

Animation, Front-End Development, 3D artist. Mark Roenfeldt is passionate in all these disciplines and skills. He is a developer with four year’s experience in animation and one year of programming. While at Full Sail University, his work in animation improved from being simplistic to complex, which reflects the quality of his work. Mark trained for the past ten years to be part of the Game Industry from building multiplayer maps in Halo Forge to the last animation in his portfolio.

Mark is no stranger when it comes to hard work. During high school, he worked on creating a two and a half hour machinima (movie made from video game graphics) which was his first experience in managing the workload and complexities of a large project. Mark taught himself how to use several Adobe products and continues learning through practice and self-determination. The overall project taught him about writing, composition, acting, troubleshooting, and even crunch time which better prepared him for work as a 3D artist and developer. Today, Mark works on additional projects during his free time putting in as much dedication as with his machinima movie.

As an animator, Mark applies his knowledge of the principles of animation and his experience in dance, acting, and martial arts to create entertaining motions of characters for the viewer to enjoy. He can quickly adapt to different projects and goes through a detailed progression throughout his work. Over the years, he has developed a keen eye for detail and is able to learn quickly. Whether he works in a team or individually, Mark follows the requirements of the project given to him and completes it to the best of his ability.
